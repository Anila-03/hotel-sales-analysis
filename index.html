Hotel Sales Analysis Using Excel

Project Overview:

This project analyzes sales trends, peak hours, and profitability for Narasimha Tiffin Centre, a small hotel business. Using Excel and Power Query, I transformed raw sales data into actionable insights, helping optimize inventory, staffing, and menu pricing.

Project Objectives:

  • Identify Sales Trends & Peak Hours – Optimize staffing and operations.
  • Analyze Best & Worst Selling Items – Determine revenue-driving items.
  • Evaluate Profit Margins – Identify high and low-profit days.
  • Find the Busiest Days of the Month – Identify peak business days.
  • Improve Inventory Management – Prevent shortages and excess waste.
  • Suggest Business Growth Strategies – Promotions, menu adjustments, and operations.

Key Insights & Findings:

  • Peak Sales Days: Jan 5, 9, 20, and 28 (~400+ plates, ₹6,200 revenue).
  • Slowest Sales Day: Jan 23 (₹4,645 revenue).
  • Top-Selling Items: Tea & Coffee (8,186 cups), Puri (796 plates), Idli (731.5 plates).
  • Dosa's Decline: Only 320 plates sold.
  • Profit Margin Analysis:
    • Highest Profit Day: Jan 22 (3.3% margin, ₹5,787 profit).
    • Lowest Profit Day: Jan 1 (0.0% margin).

Tools & Technologies Used:

  • Microsoft Excel: Data cleaning, formulas, pivot tables.
  • Power Query: Data transformation & automation.
  • Data Visualization: Charts for trend analysis.

Next Steps & Improvements:

  • Develop an inventory forecasting model.
